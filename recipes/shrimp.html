Shrimp Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • Shrimp
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and seasonings in one bowl. Beat the eggs until frothy in a second bowl. Place the bread crumbs in a third bowl.
  2. Dredge the shrimp in the flour mixture, then dip it into the eggs. Finally, press the shrimp in the crumbs. Turn to coat.
  3. Heat oil in a heavy pot to 350 degrees F. Deep fry the shrimp in batches until cooked through. Transfer to paper towels to drain.
